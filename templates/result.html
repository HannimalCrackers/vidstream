Saturn has Taken Your Skin


Your skin  has been wrapped around   Saturn   like a terrible planetary flesh horror.

And in exchange  the planet has   Saturnized   your face. Take a  good look. That is   your skin. And this  is  you.


~HAPPY HALLOWEEN 2021~




This is a spooky project by Bill and Hannah of NYC Resistor, using the CycleGAN style transfer machine learning model.
NYC Resistor is a Brooklyn-based community of makers, artists, and citizen scientists. You can support much nicer work through our Withfriends.
